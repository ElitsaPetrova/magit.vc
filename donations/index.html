One-time donations

PayPal

PayPal allows donating using a PayPal account, a credit card, or a bank account. Regardless of what method you want to use, follow this link or the button above. Then either login to your PayPal account using the login dialog on the right or click on the small "Continue" link on the left of the login dialog to donate using your credit card or bank account.

Make sure you DO NOT mark Make This Recurring (Monthly), unless of course that's what you want.

Payoneer

Payoneer allows donating using a credit card. Follow this link and then enter jonas@bernoul.li as Recipient's Email and complete the subsequent steps.

Coinbase (Bitcoin)

Donate Bitcoins
by clicking on the above button. You can either send your donation to the displayed address or if you have a Coinbase wallet, then you can use that instead.

If you always wanted to get some Bitcoins but just never got around to doing so until now, then you might want to consider getting some using Coinbase by following this link. If you do that and buy bitcoins for $100 or more, then Coinbase will pay me $10. So far I am happy with Coinbase, I hope you will too. It was easy to get francs into and out of Coinbase.

Monthly subscriptions

PayPal

PayPal allows donating using a PayPal account, a credit card, or a bank account. Regardless of what method you want to use, follow this link or the button above. Then either login to your PayPal account using the login dialog on the right or click on the small "Continue" link on the left of the login dialog to donate using your credit card or bank account.

Make sure you DO mark Make This Recurring (Monthly), unless of course you want to make a one-time donation.

Patreon

Patreon allows making monthly donations. You'll have to create an account and add money to it. Follow this link to setup your monthly donation.

Gratipay

Gratipay allows making monthly donations. You'll have to create an account and add money to it. Follow this link to setupyour monthly donation.

Flattr

Flattr allows making monthly donations. You'll have to create an account and add money to it. Follow this link or the button above to setup your monthly donation.

Bountysource Salt

Bountysource Salt allows making monthly donations. You'll have to create an account and add money to it. Follow this link to setup your monthly donation.

Coinbase (Bitcoin)

Donate Bitcoins
by clicking on the above button. The entered amount will be payed from your Coinbase wallet every month until you cancel the subscription.

You need a Coinbase wallet to do so. If you would like send Bitcoins as intended by Satoshi, then you'll have to make a one-time donation (of course you are welcome to do so every month :-).

Issue Bounties

Bountysource

Bountysource allows backing particular issues you are interested in. You'll have to create an account and add money to it.

Because I have done a good job preventing a large backlog, there currently aren't many issues you could back, but feel free to create and back new issues.

To back issues follow this link.
